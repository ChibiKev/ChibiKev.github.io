Find It Quick

Project Information

  • Category: Web
  • Project Date: September 2019 - December 2019
  • Project URL: https://github.com/Special-Team-7/Find-It-Quick

Find It Quick is a responsive full-stack web application designed to assist urban dwellers in quickly locating public restrooms.

It was developed with React, Node + Express, and PostgreSQL and it is an information and rating-based web application, akin to platforms like Yelp and Waze. Restrooms are categorized and filtered based on their accessibility, such as public, mall, park, or customers-only facilities, and the application also provides insights into restroom congestion levels.

The inspiration for this application arises from real-world scenarios in urban environments, where finding a restroom can be a challenging task without incurring expenses or waiting in long queues. Find It Quick is primarily tailored to cater to individuals who urgently need restroom facilities or face difficulties in managing such needs.
