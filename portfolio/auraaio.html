Aura.AIO

Project Information

  • Category: Software
  • Project Date: March 2021 - Present (Hold)
  • Project URL: https://github.com/JK-Productions/Aura

Aura.AIO is an Electron-based application that automates the process of online shopping.

Aura.AIO allows users to create accounts, securely store personal data like payment methods, shipping addresses, and billing information, and fully customize their purchase configurations. They can also designate stores for automation and streamline product acquisition through keyword-based searches.

The driving force behind this project is to simplify the process of swiftly acquiring highly sought-after products upon their release.
