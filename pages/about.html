Biography

Hello! I'm Kevin

I graduated from The City College of New York with a Bachelor of Engineering. I have always been fascinated by technology ever since I was little. My inspiration grew during my last year of high school when I took a class that discussed HTML and CSS. During this time, I also discovered a passion for PC building which led me to pursue a major in Computer Engineering at City College. Throughout my time at City College, I have coded many projects using different programming languages such as C/C++, Java, Javascript, and Python and have worked on various hands-on circuit designs.

I was also part of a year long technical and professional development program known as CUNY Tech Prep. While at CUNY Tech Prep, I built two web applications, Find It Quick and Split It, where I learned React, Node + Express, and PostgreSQL. My experiences at CUNY Tech Prep motivated me to continue pursuing web development by working on numerous school projects and personal projects. I later graduated from City College with a degree in Computer Engineering and a minor in Mathematics. My skills strongly lie around Full Stack Development, but I am always looking for opportunities for growth and experimenting with different technologies. I am excited to apply the knowledge gained from my experiences into real world applications and connecting with people who share similar interests with me such as creating new technologies. In my free time, I enjoy strengthening my skills by working on new projects and teaching others what I learned.
